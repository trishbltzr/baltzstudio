### Without Cocoon Consult (you build it)

1. **Share the audit** — Bring the findings to your team, agency, or build partner. Everything is already mapped.
2. **Turn priorities into a brief** — Translate the audit into what gets built, what gets fixed, and what waits.
3. **Align offer, pages, and CTA** — Structure the site around the offer and audience — not just the layout.
4. **Translate direction into wireframes** — Shape page flow before design starts, so design has something to build on.
5. **Write or revise core copy** — Update headlines, sections, and CTAs to match the agreed direction.
6. **Choose visuals from the strategy** — Colors, fonts, imagery — chosen from the brief, not personal taste.
7. **Keep feedback tied to the audit** — Review decisions against the brief, not preference.
8. **Manage scope** — Decide what belongs in this build, what waits, and who makes the call.
9. **QA against the strategy** — Check mobile, flow, copy, and conversion against what the site needs to do.
10. **Launch with the brief as reference** — The Cocoon Consult direction is the source of truth for final decisions.
