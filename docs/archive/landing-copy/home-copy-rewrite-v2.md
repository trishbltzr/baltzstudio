### Without Cocoon Consult (you build it)

1. **Share the audit** — Bring the findings to your team, agency, or build partner. Everything is already mapped.
2. **Turn priorities into a brief** — Translate the audit into what gets fixed, built, protected, and what waits.
3. **Align offer, pages, and CTA** — Make sure the site structure supports the offer and audience — not just the layout.
4. **Translate direction into wireframes** — Shape page flow before design starts, so design has something solid to build on.
5. **Write or revise core copy** — Update headlines, sections, and CTAs to match the agreed direction.
6. **Choose visuals from the strategy** — Select colors, fonts, and imagery that support the brief — not personal taste.
7. **Keep feedback tied to the audit** — Review decisions against the brief, not preference.
8. **Manage scope** — Decide what belongs in this build, what waits, and who makes the call.
9. **QA against the strategy** — Check mobile, flow, copy, and conversion against what the site needs to do.
10. **Launch with the brief as reference** — Use the Cocoon Consult direction as the source of truth for final decisions.

### With Cocoon Consult (we build it)

1. **Cocoon Consult™** — Message, offer, audience, proof, structure, and scope get decided before design starts.
2. **Winged in a Week™** — The approved strategy becomes page structure, design direction, build, and QA — in one week, start to finish.
3. **In Full Flight™** — Launch gets handled. After that, updates go through a clear process instead of landing in your inbox.
